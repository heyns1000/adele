About the Journey

Adele's story is one of profound courage. From a place of darkness, she has found the strength to stand tall, embracing her creative gifts and professional expertise to build a new life. This portfolio is a monument to that hope, a showcase of a mind that finds beauty and narrative in every challenge.

"Where others see an ending, I see the opportunity for a new beginning, beautifully designed."
